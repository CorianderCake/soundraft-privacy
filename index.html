Privacy Policy – Soundraft (Chrome Extension)

Effective Date: April 2, 2025

Soundraft does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data. All functionality runs entirely within the browser. The extension does not access or transmit personal information, browsing activity, or media content.

Soundraft does not use cookies, analytics, trackers, or any third-party services. No user behavior is monitored or reported. All audio is processed locally and temporarily using the Web Audio API.

In future versions, metadata sharing may be implemented to enable project sharing. If so, this policy will be updated accordingly and reviewed before rollout.
